## ⚠️ Important Notice

When updating the mod, **always remove the previous recipes**!

Go to the directory:  
📂 `mod/kubejs/server_scripts/CreateMechanisms/`  
or  
📂 `mod/kubejs/server_scripts/CreateMechanismsBase/`

Delete the **CreateMechanisms** or **CreateMechanismsBase** folder before launching the game.

A new folder will be automatically created with the updated recipes, ensuring the mod works correctly.

## 🛠️ New Items

### Saw 🔧🌲
A versatile tool used to cut down entire trees and start mechanisms. Its efficiency increases with level, making wood processing faster and more effective.

### Rubber 🏭🛠️
Two main types: **traditional** and **enderiam**. Each has a **cured version**, totaling four different items.  
Main use: fluid and heat mechanisms.

### Bronze 🔥⚙️
Base material for heat mechanisms, essential for machines operating at high temperatures.

### Energy Cells ⚡📦
It involves two levels traditional cell and enderiam and the concept is applied in the construction of electrical mechanisms.

## New Mechanisms ⚙️✨

This mod adds **7 new mechanisms** to the Create mod, expanding the possibilities for developers and players to create innovative experiences. These mechanisms are designed to increase the automation, engineering, and processing options in the Create universe, enabling the creation of more complex and efficient machines. 🚀

### 1\. **Wooden Mechanism** 🌿⚙️

The Wooden Mechanism is the basic mechanism, serving as the foundation for creating simple machines and as the structural base for most of the other mechanisms in the mod. Essential to starting your journey into automation, it is a key component in the development of more advanced systems! 🚀

### 2\. **Rubber Mechanism** 🏭💧

The Rubber Mechanism is the basis for copper-based machines and devices that operate with liquids. Essential for fluid handling and transport systems, it enables the creation of new machines specialized in hydraulic and chemical engineering! ⚙️🚀

### 3\. **Zinc Mechanism** 🔩📦

Zinc Mechanism is essential for logistics systems, being used in the creation of machines and devices aimed at transporting, organizing, and automating resources. Essential for optimizing material flows and improving the efficiency of your factory! 🚚⚙️

### 4\. **Redstone Mechanism** 🔴⚡

The **Redstone Mechanism** is a specialized component for **automation and logic control systems**. It serves as the foundation for machines that utilize **advanced Redstone** signals, making it ideal for creating smart devices, binary logic systems, flow management, and highly **customizable automation**.

If you want to control your factory with precise signals, timers, sensors, or conditional logic, the Redstone Mechanism will be your essential tool! 🧠⚡

### 5\. **Storage Mechanisms** 📦
Designed to manage and expand inventory systems, these mechanisms allow efficient storage, retrieval, and organization of resources across your factory.

### 6\. **Fluid Mechanisms** 💧
Specialized in handling liquids, these mechanisms enable pumping, transferring, and processing of fluids. Essential for chemical engineering and hydraulic systems.

### 7\. **Heat Mechanisms** 🔥
Built for machines that operate at high temperatures, these mechanisms ensure thermal control, heating, and smelting efficiency in industrial processes.

### 8\. **Energy Mechanisms** ⚡
Core components for electrical machines, ranging from basic energy transfer to advanced power grids. Each tier increases efficiency and scalability.

### 9\. **Ender-Level Mechanisms** 🌌
Available for all mechanism types, these advanced versions incorporate **Enderiam technology**.  
Examples:
- **Fluid Enderiam Mechanism** – transfers fluids using Ender energy or wireless systems.
- **Energy Enderiam Mechanism** – enables long-distance, cross-dimensional power transmission.  
  These mechanisms represent the pinnacle of automation, allowing interdimensional connectivity and mobility.

### 🌟 And Many More Mechanisms

Beyond the categories listed above, the mod offers **over 40+ unique mechanisms** to explore.  
From technical systems to magical integrations, each mechanism expands the possibilities of automation, engineering, and creativity in Minecraft.

Whether building advanced factories, experimenting with fluid dynamics, managing power grids, or exploring interdimensional connections, there is always a new mechanism waiting to be manufactured. 🚀✨

## Mod Recipe Settings ⚙️🔧

To disable the add-on mechanisms:

1.  Open the 'createmechanisms-common' mod settings page.
2.  Find and edit the 'enableAddonRequirement' setting, setting its value to `true`.
    *   This indicates that the add-on mechanisms will be generated.
3.  After making the changes, restart the modpack to apply the new settings correctly.
